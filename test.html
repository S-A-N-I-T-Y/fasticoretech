In consideration of the terms and covenants of this agreement, and other valuable consideration, the parties agree as follows:

1. MATTERS TO BE SUBMITTED TO ARBITRATION

All disputes and controversies of every kind and nature between the parties to this agreement arising out of or in connection with [SPECIFY GENERAL AGREEMENT TO WHICH ARBITRATION AGREEMENT RELATES], as to the existence, contsruction, validity, interpolation, or meaning performance, nonperformance, enforcement, operation, breach, continuance or termination of the agreement shalll be submitted to arbitration to the procedure set forth in this agreeement.

2. PROCEDURE

Either party may demand such arbitration in writing within [NUMBER] days after the controversy arises. which demand shall include the name of the arbitrator appointed by the party demanding arbitration together with a statement

a. The arbitration hearing shall be held within [NUMBER] days after the appointment of the third arbitrator.

b. The arbitrators shall appoint a time and place for the hearing and cause notification to the parties to be served personally or by registered mail not less than [NUMBER] days before the hearing.

c. The arbitrators shall hear and determine the controversy upon the evidence produced notwithstanding the failure of a party duly notified to appear. The hearing may be adjourned from time to time as necessary and on appropriate notice to the absent party.

d. The arbitrators shall have [NUMBER] days from the date of the closing of the hearings to make their decision. The decision shall be in writing and a copy thereof shall be delivered to each of the parties personally or by registered mail.

e. Any award rendered by the arbitrators pursuant to this agreement shall be final, conclusive, and binding upon the parties, and a judgment thereon may be entered in the highest court of the forum, State or Federal, having jurisdiction.
